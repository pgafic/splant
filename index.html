PGA Green Global Solutions Co.

PGA Green Global Solutions Co., based in Jaro, Iloilo City, is a leader in waterproofing and general construction, serving Western Visayas. The company specializes in residential and industrial projects, offering services such as waterproofing, tank lining, crack injection, floor coating, finishing, repainting, and more. Additionally, PGA provides personal protective equipment (PPE) to ensure safety and compliance.

Certified by Pioneer Pro Adhesives & Waterproofing Products in 2016 as the official applicator in Western Visayas, PGA upholds industry standards with certifications from BIR and SEC. Their mission focuses on delivering high-quality solutions while building strong client partnerships.

PGA is also the exclusive regional distributor of Plantex All-in-One, a flagship product offering eco-friendly cleaning solutions through organic products.

Your Safety is Our First Priority

First Industry Company is a sister company of PGA, and FIC focuses on implementing fire safety and security. FIC offers a range of services, including the installation and inspection of Fire Detection and Alarm Systems, as well as the refilling of fire extinguishers. Its flagship products include the Fire 101 Extinguishers, which are high-quality, non-toxic, and environmentally friendly, designed to combat various types of fires effectively.
